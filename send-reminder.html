Automatic Reminders

Ding Dong! The delivery is just a step away from arriving.
Reminder emails will be Automatically Sent to the customer with orders that are in the 'Processing' or 'Completed' status.

Manual Email Reminders

You may send an email notification to all customers who have a future delivery. This will use the default template specified under WooCommerce > Settings > Emails.

You can insert the following tags. They will be replaced dynamically: {order_date} {order_number} {customer_name} {customer_first_name} {customer_last_name} {delivery_date} {delivery_time} {order_details}
